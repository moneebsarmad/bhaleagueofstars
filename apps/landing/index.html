What This Is

A Framework for Formation

This is a school-wide identity framework designed to shape student culture across both Middle and High School.

1 One League per school
4 Four Houses per League
→ Used consistently across grades

The language stays the same.

How It Works

One System, Shared Identity

One League per school
Four Houses per League
Every student belongs to one House
Houses are training areas, not labels
Students grow within their House over time

The goal is not competition.
The goal is shared identity and growth.

Decision Point

The Two League Types

This choice sets the tone for the entire system.

  • How students respond to difficulty

Understanding Houses

What Houses Are — And Aren't

Houses are:

  • Areas of focused growth
  • A shared language for encouragement
  • A framework for formation

Houses are not:

  • Personality types
  • Rankings
  • Labels of worth

Students don't earn Houses.
They grow within them.

Build Culture Intentionally

A strong school culture doesn't come from rules alone.
It comes from shared language, shared values, and consistent expectations.

This system gives schools a way to build that — intentionally.
